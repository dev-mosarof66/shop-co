# E-Commerce Template - Features Documentation

## 📋 Overview
A professional, fully-customizable e-commerce template designed for businesses of all sizes to quickly launch their online store with their own branding, logo, and products.

---

## 🎨 Customization & Branding Features

### Brand Customization
- **Logo Upload**: Easy logo upload and placement options (header, footer, favicon)
- **Color Scheme**: Customizable color palette for entire site (primary, secondary, accent colors)
- **Typography**: Font family selection and sizing options
- **Favicon & Metadata**: Custom favicon, meta tags, and SEO titles
- **Domain Configuration**: Custom domain name support
- **Email Templates**: Customizable email designs with brand colors

### Layout Customization
- **Homepage Layout**: Drag-and-drop homepage builder with predefined sections
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile, tablet, and desktop optimization
- **Footer Customization**: Custom links, company info, social media links
- **Banner Management**: Custom promotional banners and hero sections
- **Theme Variations**: Multiple pre-built professional themes to choose from

## 🛍️ Product Management Features

### Product Creation & Management
- **Product Upload**: Single and bulk product upload (CSV/Excel import)
- **Product Variants**: Size, color, material, and custom attribute variations
- **Product Images**: Multiple image uploads with zoom functionality, gallery view
- **Product Description**: Rich text editor with formatted descriptions
- **SKU Management**: Unique SKU tracking for inventory
- **Pricing Tiers**: Volume discounts and tiered pricing options
- **Product Categories**: Nested category management
- **Product Tags**: Tagging system for better search and filtering
- **Product Status**: Draft, active, inactive product management

### Product Details
- **Product Specifications**: Custom fields for detailed specifications
- **Digital Products**: Support for downloadable files and digital goods
- **Physical Products**: Weight, dimensions, shipping information
- **Related Products**: Cross-sell and upsell product recommendations
- **Product History**: Version control and change tracking
